Application requirement:
  • Hong Kong Permanent Resident or possesses company employment contract
  • Annual household income of no less than HK$180,000 (monthly salary of HK$15,000)
  • The minimum allowable wage of Domestic Helper is HK$4,410
  • Employers should provide the Helper with suitable accommodation
  • Employer shall provide free return air ticket to the Helper
  • Fair language abilities for communication
  • Both employers and employees must sign the standard 2-year contract (I.D.407)

Domestic Helpers are entitled to:
  • Rest day of 1 day each week, excluding official holidays.
  • Statutory holidays of 12 days per annum.
  • Free medical care;
  • Paid sick leave of 2 days per month in the first year;
  • Severance payment

Reason for termination:
  • Inability to communicate in English
  • Poor performance (Laziness or poor working experience)
  • Dishonesty and theft
  • Lack of proper personal hygiene
  • Lack of common sense
  • Homesickness
  • Physically unhealthy
